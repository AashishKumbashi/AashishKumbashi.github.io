Call for Papers

We invite submissions of original research, insightful case studies, and work in progress that address XAI applications within Ubiquitous and Wearable Computing. Submissions should be up to 4 pages (including references) and will be reviewed through a double-blind process. Accepted papers will be published in the ACM Digital Library and the Adjunct Proceedings.
